Portfolio

Wishlist

Wishlist is an app that allows users to record restaurants they want to visit. Plugging into the Google Maps and Google Places APIs, the app automatically stores a restaurant's crucial information like location and hours, while also allowing the user to add their own notes based on friend's recommendations or their own personal experiences. Never again forget the name of that great spot your friend said you have to try!


Built in: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node JS, MongoDB

Fantasy Congress 2018

Fantasy Congress 2018 is an app that allows users to engage in the American political process in a fun, informed way. With the 2018 Congressional Races on the horizon, the passive observer will only hear about Donald Trump's crazy Tweets. Fantasy Congress 2018 gives users a good reason to dig into the data and opinion polls, to put together the best team of candidates that will score you the most points on election night. Challenge friends and family to see who really knows more about the state of our nation.


Built in: React, Redux, JavaScript, Node JS, MongoDB
